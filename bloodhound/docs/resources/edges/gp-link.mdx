The GPLink relationship connects a Group Policy Object ([GPO](https://github.com/SpecterOps/bloodhound-docs/blob/main//resources/nodes/gpo)) to an AD domain or organizational unit ([OU](https://github.com/SpecterOps/bloodhound-docs/blob/main//resources/nodes/ou)) it is linked to.

The [GPO](https://github.com/SpecterOps/bloodhound-docs/blob/main//resources/nodes/gpo)'s settings apply to AD accounts (users and computers) within that domain or [OU](https://github.com/SpecterOps/bloodhound-docs/blob/main//resources/nodes/ou).

The Enforced property on the edge indicates whether the [GPO](https://github.com/SpecterOps/bloodhound-docs/blob/main//resources/nodes/gpo) link is enforced, meaning it still applies if an [OU](https://github.com/SpecterOps/bloodhound-docs/blob/main//resources/nodes/ou) has blocked GPO inheritance.

## Abuse Info

Control over the [GPO](https://github.com/SpecterOps/bloodhound-docs/blob/main//resources/nodes/gpo) can be abused to compromise the AD accounts the GPO applies to by modifying the GPO policy settings.
