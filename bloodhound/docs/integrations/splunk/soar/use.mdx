After installation and configuration are complete, you can view attack path findings from BloodHound Enterprise in Splunk SOAR. This allows you to monitor potential attack paths in real-time and trigger automated response playbooks based on BloodHound Enterprise detections.

To view attack path findings from BloodHound Enterprise in Splunk SOAR:

1. Log in to your Splunk SOAR instance.
1. Click the **Events** tab to view all polled events.
