## Edge Schema

- Source: [Okta_Device](https://github.com/SpecterOps/bloodhound-docs/blob/main//opengraph/extensions/okta/nodes/okta_device)
- Destination: [Okta_User](https://github.com/SpecterOps/bloodhound-docs/blob/main//opengraph/extensions/okta/nodes/okta_user)
- Traversable: ❌

## General Information

The non-traversable Okta_DeviceOf edges represent the ownership relationships between users and devices in Okta:


```mermaid
graph LR
    u1("Okta_User john\@contoso.com")
    u2("Okta_User steve\@contoso.com")
    d1("Okta_Device John's MacBook")
    d2("Okta_Device Steve's iPhone")
    d1 -. Okta_DeviceOf .-> u1
    d1 -. Okta_DeviceOf .-> u2
    d2 -. Okta_DeviceOf .-> u2
```
